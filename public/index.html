Unveiling the RED Planet's Secrets

Taylor McWilliam circa 2025

Welcome to the Mars Crater Explorer, a cutting-edge project that transforms raw hyperspectral imagery from Mars into interactive 3D models and detailed crater analyses. Using data from THEMIS (Thermal Emission Imaging System), this initiative converts massive PGM files into optimized 3D terrain models (OBJ and GLB formats) for web visualization, while also detecting and analyzing Martian craters with advanced statistical tools. Whether you're a scientist, educator, or space enthusiast, explore our hosted 3D models on Clooned, download crater datasets, or dive into the code to uncover the geological stories etched into Mars’ surface—all powered by open-source technology and a passion for discovery.
